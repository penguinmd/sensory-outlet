Our Services

How we support individuals with sensory needs

Workshops & Training

We offer regular workshops for parents, educators, and community members on understanding and supporting sensory processing needs. Our workshops cover topics such as:

  • Introduction to Sensory Processing
  • Creating Sensory-Friendly Environments
  • Sensory Strategies for Home and School
  • Supporting Sensory Needs in Public Spaces

Workshops are led by trained professionals and include both information sessions and hands-on activities.

Resource Library

Our resource library offers a collection of books, tools, and equipment related to sensory processing. Community members can:

  • Borrow sensory tools to try at home before purchasing
  • Access educational resources and books
  • Receive guidance on appropriate sensory tools for specific needs

Our library operates on a membership basis, with sliding scale fees to ensure accessibility for all.

Support Groups

We facilitate regular support groups for:

  • Parents of children with sensory processing differences
  • Adults with sensory sensitivities
  • Educators working with students with sensory needs

These groups provide a space for sharing experiences, strategies, and mutual support.

Community Events

We organize sensory-friendly community events throughout the year, including:

  • Sensory-friendly movie screenings
  • Inclusive play dates
  • Sensory-aware community outings
  • Awareness and advocacy events

All events are designed with sensory needs in mind, providing accommodations and support as needed.

Consultations

We offer consultations for:

  • Businesses seeking to create more sensory-friendly environments
  • Community organizations planning inclusive events
  • Schools working to better support students with sensory needs

Consultations include assessment, recommendations, and follow-up support.
