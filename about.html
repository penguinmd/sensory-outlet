About Us

Learn about our organization and mission

Our Story

Sensory Outlet was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to provide resources and support for individuals with sensory processing needs. Our organization began as a small community group and has since grown to serve hundreds of people in our area.

Our founders recognized the gap in services for people with sensory sensitivities and decided to create a space where individuals and families could find resources, community, and understanding.

Our Mission

At Sensory Outlet, our mission is to enhance the quality of life for individuals with sensory processing differences through education, resources, and community support.

We strive to create a world where sensory needs are recognized, understood, and accommodated in all aspects of daily life.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity: We welcome everyone, regardless of ability or background.
  • Empowerment: We provide tools and knowledge that help individuals and families thrive.
  • Community: We foster connections and relationships among those we serve.
  • Advocacy: We work to increase awareness and understanding of sensory processing needs.

Our Team

Our dedicated team consists of professionals with backgrounds in occupational therapy, education, and community organizing. We are united by our passion for supporting individuals with sensory processing differences.

We also rely on the support of many wonderful volunteers who contribute their time and talents to our programs and events.
